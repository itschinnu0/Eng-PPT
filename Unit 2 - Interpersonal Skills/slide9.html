Grammar: Sentence Patterns

Four essential structures to build variety, precision, and flow in professional engineering communication.

01

Simple

Contains one independent clause. Direct and specific statements.

"The engineer solved the problem."

[Indep. Clause]

02

Compound

Two independent clauses connected by a conjunction (and, but, or).

"...and the team reviewed findings."

[Indep] + [Conj] + [Indep]

03

Complex

Independent clause + dependent clause. Shows relationships.

"...because the team provided data."

[Indep] + [Dep]

04

Cmpd-Complex

Two+ independent clauses and at least one dependent clause.

"I coded it, and she tested it while I watched."

[2+ Indep] + [1+ Dep]
