Using Idioms & Phrases Effectively

Real-World Applications

Expressive Idioms

Make interactions more engaging.
"Let's get the ball rolling" (start the project) or "Break the ice" (initiate conversation).

Technical Phrasal Verbs

Standard in engineering reports.
Use "carry out" (perform), "set up" (configure), or "look into" (investigate).

Professional Collocations

Natural word pairings for fluency.
Examples: "develop a solution", "face challenges", or "meet a deadline".
