Speaking Skills

Technical Presentations

Communicating complex information clearly to diverse audiences by structuring data and adapting technical depth.

01

Structured Clarity

Presenting detailed technical information in a logical, organized format that guides the listener through complex concepts step-by-step.

Strategy

"Use a 'Problem-Solution-Impact' framework."

Organize by logic, not just chronology

02

Audience Adaptation

Tailoring language, tone, and technical depth based on the audience's expertise level to ensure maximum comprehension and engagement.

Strategy

"Adjusting jargon usage."

High-level for execs vs. Deep-dive for peers

03

Mixed Audience Scenario

Bridging the gap effectively when addressing groups containing both subject matter experts and non-technical stakeholders simultaneously.

Real World Example

"Presenting AI algorithms at a conference."

Explain logic simply, then show code proof
