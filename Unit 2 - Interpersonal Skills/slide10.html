Strategic Writing

Grammar:
When to Use Each Pattern

Simple Sentences

Use for direct, specific information such as emails, instructions, or emphatic statements.

Compound Sentences

Best for connecting related ideas of equal importance to show balance and continuity.

Complex & Compound-Complex

Essential for explaining interrelated ideas, causes, conditions, and technical processes.

Key Benefit

Varied sentence structure improves readability, clarity, and audience engagement in technical reports.
