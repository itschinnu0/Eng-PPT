Professional Communication

Email Etiquette & Structure

Essential components for clear and professional digital correspondence.

Clear Subject Line

Concise, descriptive summary of the purpose. Avoid vague titles like "Hello".

Polite Greeting

Formal salutation (e.g., "Dear Mr. Smith," "Hi Team,") appropriate for the audience.

Focused Body

Brief and direct. One main idea per paragraph. Use bullet points for readability.

Professional Closing & Signature

Courteous sign-off ("Best regards") followed by full contact details.
