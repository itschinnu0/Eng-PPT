Identify and correct typos or misspelled technical terms. Use tools but verify manually.

Review Grammar

Ensure subject-verb agreement, correct tense usage, and proper sentence structure.

Verify Punctuation

Check commas, periods, and colons. Correct punctuation clarifies meaning and tone.

Formatting & Consistency

Ensure consistent fonts, headings, and spacing. A clean layout improves readability.
