Speaking Skills

Questions & Requests

Essential structures for gathering information, seeking assistance, and confirming details in professional settings.

Yes/No Questions

Direct questions that expect a simple affirmation or denial. Used to confirm facts or status.

"Is the report ready?"

Use: Checking status

"Did you complete the design?"

Use: Verifying completion

Polite Requests

Courteous ways to ask for assistance, favors, or action using modal verbs like could or would.

"Could you share the file?"

Use: Asking for resources

"Would you mind reviewing this?"

Use: Asking for effort

Question Tags

Short phrases added to statements to invite agreement or confirm understanding.

"You've reviewed the report, haven't you?"

Use: Seeking confirmation

"The data is accurate, isn't it?"

Use: Inviting agreement
