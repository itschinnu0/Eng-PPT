Digital Professionalism

Netiquette & Social Media Behavior

Guidelines for maintaining a professional digital presence.

Do's

Best Practices

Be Respectful & Professional

Always maintain a polite tone and treat others with respect, regardless of the platform.

Use Clear Subject Lines

Ensure email subjects and post titles clearly indicate the content and purpose.

Share Relevant Content

Post content that adds value to professional discussions and aligns with your industry.

Protect Privacy

Respect data privacy for yourself and others. Avoid sharing sensitive company information.

Don'ts

Behaviors to Avoid

Use Rude Language

Avoid offensive, aggressive, or unprofessional language, even in informal channels.

Spam or Overshare

Don't clutter channels with irrelevant messages, excessive promotional content, or personal drama.

Ignore Context

Don't use informal slang in formal emails or treat professional forums like personal social media.

Post in Anger

Never post or reply when emotional. Wait, review, and respond calmly to avoid regret.
