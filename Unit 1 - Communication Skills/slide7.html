Sentence Structures for Engineers

Three distinct patterns to ensure clarity, precision, and professional flow in technical documentation.

01

Simple Sentence

Contains a single independent clause with one subject and one predicate. Ideal for direct, concise statements.

Example

"The engineer calculated the data."

[Subject] + [Verb]

02

Compound Sentence

Joins two independent clauses with a coordinating conjunction (FANBOYS) or semicolon. Connects related ideas.

Example

"...and the manager approved."

[Clause 1] + [Conjunction] + [Clause 2]

03

Complex Sentence

Combines an independent clause with one or more dependent clauses. Shows relationships like cause/effect.

Example

"...because the client requested it."

[Indep. Clause] + [Dep. Clause]
