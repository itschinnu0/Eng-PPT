Employability Skills

Writing: Resume/CV

Key elements for crafting a professional and impactful curriculum vitae.

Concise Length

Limit to 1-2 pages. Recruiters spend an average of 6-7 seconds scanning a resume.

Essential Sections

Include Contact Info, Summary, Skills, Experience, Education, and relevant Projects.

Tailored Content

Use keywords from the job description to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Clean Layout

Use bullet points, consistent fonts, and whitespace to ensure high readability.
