Writing: Job Applications (Cover Letters)

A step-by-step guide to crafting persuasive cover letters that secure interviews.

1

The Introduction

Clearly state who you are and the specific role you are applying for. Hook the reader immediately with a strong opening statement.

Focus: Identity & Intent

2

Show Value & Fit

Explain why you fit the role by connecting your skills to their needs. Demonstrate specific value through past achievements.

Focus: Relevance & Impact

3

Closing & Attachments

Reiterate your enthusiasm, propose a next step (interview), and ensure all required documents are attached before sending.

Focus: Action & Completion

Critical Success Factor: Tailoring

Avoid generic templates. Customize the content to reflect the company's specific culture and requirements.
