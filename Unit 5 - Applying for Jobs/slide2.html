Speaking: Mock & Telephone Interviews

Strategies for mastering high-stakes professional conversations

Mock Interviews

Practice Questions: Rehearse common HR and technical questions to formulate clear answers.

Reduce Anxiety: Familiarity with the format helps lower stress levels and builds confidence.

Telephone Interviews

Vocal Clarity: Focus on a clear, steady voice and professional tone without visual cues.

Concise Answers: Deliver focused responses to maintain engagement over the phone.
